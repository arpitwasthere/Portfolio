ABOUT ME

My name is Arpit Saini, and I am currently pursuing a B.VOC (VFA) degree from Arena Animation, Kamla Nagar. I am a passionate Graphic Designer and Video Compositor with a keen eye for detail and creativity. Over time, I have developed strong skills in creating compelling visuals and blending artistic ideas with technical expertise. My journey in the creative field has taught me the value of storytelling, teamwork, and constant learning.

As I continue to grow in this ever-evolving industry, I am eager to explore new challenges and push the boundaries of my creativity. My ultimate goal is to establish myself as a VFX Designer, where I can bring imaginative visuals to life and create immersive experiences for audiences worldwide. I believe that with dedication and hard work, I can make a meaningful impact in the world of visual effects and animation.
